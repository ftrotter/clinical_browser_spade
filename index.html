How we anonymize your browser history...

Its simple, Wikipedia codes its websites with clinical ontologies. We search your browser history and we only include searches that included visits to clinical pages... and we throw away everything else. But why trust us... click the sourcecode link on the main menu to take a look at the sourcecode for this website. You can also look at our data about which parts of wikipedia are clinical. Most importantly, we show you the results of our process our filtering.. so you can manually exclude anything you like from the ultimate upload!
